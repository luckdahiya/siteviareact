ABOUT INDIAN CUISINE

Welcome to Sizzle and Spice, your destination for authentic Indian cuisine, available exclusively for takeout and delivery. We are passionate about bringing you the rich sizzle and spices of India, crafted with the finest ingredients and traditional recipes. From classic dishes like Butter Chicken and Paneer Tikka to our signature Amritsari Aalu Kulcha with Chana, our menu is designed to delight your taste buds. At Sizzle and Spice, we believe in delivering an unforgettable culinary experience right to your doorstep. Join us on this flavorful journey and enjoy the best of Indian cuisine in the comfort of your home.
